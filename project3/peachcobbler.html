Peach Cobbler

Ingredients:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Mix peaches, 1/4 cup of sugar, lemon juice, and cinnamon in a large bowl.


2. Pour melted butter into 9"x13" glass baking dish, evenly coating the bottom and sides.


3. Whisk flour, remaining sugar, baking powder, and salt in another large bowl. Add milk, stirring until just moist.


4. Pour batter into prepared baking dish. Distribute peaches evenly over batter, pouring over any remaining juice.


5. Bake until golden brown, about 45 minutes. Serve warm.


Tip!

The batter for this cobbler pairs great with any fruit. I like to add blackberries along with peaches, but I've also used this recipe to make cherry, plum, and other stone fruit/berry cobblers.

Whatever fruit you use to make this, serve with fresh whipped cream (from my banana cream pie recipe), or vanilla ice cream.
