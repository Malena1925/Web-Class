Art of Baking

Banana Bread

Ingredients:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease and flour a 9"x5" loaf pan. Toast walnut pieces on a skillet, stirrng continuously so they don't burn.


2. In a large mixing bowl, cream softened butter and sugar.


3. Mash bananas with a fork until the consistency of chunky applesauce. Add them to the butter-sugar mixture, along with two eggs and vanilla extract. Mix until combined.


4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt. Gently fold into wet ingredients.


5. Stir in walnuts and chocolate (if using). Pour batter into prepared loaf pan. Bake for abour an hour, or until toothpick inserted into center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Let banana bread rest for 10 minutes to allow for residual cooking before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.


Tip!

If you don't have very ripe bananas on hand but still want banana bread, place the bananas on a baking sheet and bake at a low temperature to make them softer and sweeter.
