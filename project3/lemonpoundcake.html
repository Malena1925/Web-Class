Art of Baking

Lemon Pound Cake

Ingredients:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease and flour a 9"x5" loaf pan.


2. Using your fingers, rub together granulated sugar and lemon zest to release their oils and intensify the flavor. Mix in flour, baking powder, and salt.


3. While mixing on low speed, drop in softened butter one tablespoon at a time. Mix until it becomes crumbly with no remaining lumps of butter.


4. Scrape down the sides of the bowl before adding in vanilla and eggs, one at a time. Once combined, add in lemon juice, followed by the milk.


5. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread evenly. Bake for 50 minutes to an hour until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean and the edges appear golden brown with a puffed center. Allow to cool for 45 minutes before removing from pan.


Tip!

While this cake is great on its own, there are plenty of ways to jazz it up.


A. Combine milk, powdered sugar, lemon zest + juice, and vanilla to make a citrusy glaze.


B. Coat one cup of blueberries with a tablespoon of flour before mixing in the batter to get a blueberry lemon loaf. This works great with any other berry too.


C. Swirl in a few tablespoons of jam.


D. Make a quick crumble of flour, butter, and sugar before baking.
