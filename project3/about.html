About


This website is a digital translation of my recipe sketchbook. Over the years, I have recorded the many recipes I have tested and developed, writing notes in the margins and sketching the end result. Each time I revisit a recipe, the page gets added to: additions, substitutions, advice, and little odds and ends tucked within. For this project, I have refined some of my chaotic spreads while trying to stay true to the nature of the original book, hiding tips and tricks throughout the site.
